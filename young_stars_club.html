young stars club

Have you ever wondered what the dinosaurs ate? What lies at the bottom of the ocean? or what a dying star is called? Here at the Young Stars Club we explore these questions and more!

Our Young Stars Club members are the researchers of tomorrow and we spare no expense to see their interest in everything around us grow.

We offer our Young Stars a free room which they can use whenever they want during our open hours to explore their scientific curiosities.

We also invite researchers from around the world to hold seminars, involve the Young Stars in experiments and have the Young Stars help in their current research.

Interested in hearing more? Send us a message.
